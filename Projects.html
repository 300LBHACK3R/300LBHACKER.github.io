Kali Anonymity Setup

Enter Kali-Anonymity-Setup—a fully automated script that turns your fresh Kali Linux install into an anonymity powerhouse. What makes it cool? Automated Anonymity: With a single command, the script sets up Tor, ProxyChains, and MAC spoofing—think of it as your own personal invisibility cloak for the internet. Easy-to-Use: Clone, chmod, and run—the setup is designed for simplicity, making it perfect for both seasoned ethical hackers and privacy enthusiasts dipping their toes into anonymity. In a world where digital privacy is paramount, Kali-Anonymity-Setup empowers you to surf, scan, and explore with confidence, all while keeping your identity under wraps.
